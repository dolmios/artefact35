DESCRIPTION

The Zenit TTL is a 35mm film SLR camera made by KMZ and produced between 1977-85. It was an upgraded version of the Zenit EM, adding stop-down TTL metering and a rewind crank in place of the knob. This camera marked the disappearance of the ghost. Nothing to see with phantoms. It was about the Russian unit of measurement of the sensibility of films. This unit was close to that of Asa.
