DESCRIPTION

The Praktica BCA is a reflex SLR camera. It was manufactured by Pentacon from 1986 to 1989 as the successor to the Praktica B 100. The lenses of the Praktica BCA are interchangeable with the Praktica bayonet mount. M42 lenses are easily adapted to this mount.
